How to Play

You are the Goblin Hunter Champion of your village and you have been sent to participate in the Valhalla Goblin Hunter Championship. Your goal is to get as many points as possible. To do so you must hunt down the Green and Blue goblins that appear while avoiding the Red ones. To move around use the Arrow Keys, it's that simple!
The Green Goblin is the easiest to hunt but it's also worth the least points. He's pretty dumb too and will stand there waiting for you to finish him off.
As you hunt down the Green Goblins you'll notice that your taste for blood will increase. Hunting enough will fill up your Blood Drops meter up to a maximum of 3 charges. Using a charge will give you a short burst of speed. To use Bloodrage, press the Space key.
The Blue Goblin is an elusive one. He's a lot smarter than his Green kin and will only show up to tease you before disappearing. The faster you hunt him down the more points you will get.

These sneaky little creatures are known to carry with them Amanita mushrooms, keep an eye out after you finish one of them off. You can hold 3 of mushrooms but picking up more will net you more points. Do not wait around to pick them up because they have a short shelf life.

The Red Goblin is mean and has a purpose: to hunt you down. Luckily he won't be around for long before needing to return to the Goblin Dimension. When you see one, run!
Like any Viking worth its salt, you know your way around fungi. The Amanita mushrooms in particular, allow you to enter a Berserker Trance. While you are under the hallucinogenic influence of this fungus you will feel no pain and be one with Valhalla. Red Goblins are now just another way to get more points but be careful because the effect is short lived. To use this ability press the Shift Key.
When the Red Goblin catches you, you'll lose one life. Lose 3 lives and the game ends. Try to avoid that.
The Red Goblin does not appreciate what you do to his fellow kin and will grow stronger as you mow down Goblins. He will appear more often, stay longer and move faster. You have been warned.
