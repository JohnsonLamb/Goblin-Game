How to Play

You are the Goblin Hunter Champion of your village and you have been sent to participate in the Valhalla Goblin Hunter Championship. Your goal is to get as many points as possible. To do so you must hunt down the Green and Blue goblins that appear while avoiding the Red ones. To move around use the Arrow Keys, it's that simple!
The Green Goblin is the easiest to hunt but it's also worth the least points. He's pretty dumb too and will stand there waiting for you to finish him off.
The Blue Goblin is an elusive one. He's a lot smarter than his Green kin and will only show up to tease you before disappearing. The faster you hunt him down the more points you will get.
The Red Goblin is mean and has a purpose: to hunt you down. Luckily he won't be around for long before needing to return to the Goblin Dimension. When you see one, run!
When the Red Goblin catches you, you'll lose one life. Lose 3 lives and the game ends. Try to avoid that.
As you hunt down the Green Goblins you'll notice that your taste for blood will increase. Hunting enough will fill up your Bloodlust meter and using it will give you a short burst of speed. To use Bloodlust, press the Space key.

Upcoming Features

Having only one use of the Bloodlust ability leads to stale gameplay. In order to make things more interesting, a Charge system will be implemented. You will then be able to store up to 3 charges of bloodlust to use at your discretion.
Having charges on the Bloodlust ability is a start but you're still left with only one ability and that is limiting. Fear not! Soon you'll be able to pick up Amanita mushrooms from fallen Blue Goblins and use them to fuel your Berserker rage. While in this trance like state you'll be able to take the Red Goblins head on for some sweet points. No more running!
You are probably thinking that with the above mentioned additions the game will become easy. Worry not fearsome warrior for in the not so distant future the difficulty will scale as you progress. The more points you accumulate the higher the chance of being harassed by the Red Goblin. He will also be able to pursue you for longer periods of time.
